A little bit about us / introduction:

The National Digital Security & Analysis Wing (NDSAW) is a student-led initiative at GHS, built to protect students from digital threats—silently, safely, and seriously.

From cyberbullying to blackmail, stalking to hacking—we step in when things go too far.
Our work is confidential, precise, and student-run. No teachers. No exposure. Just quiet action.
You stay protected. We stay anonymous. And the game ain't over until I win.
